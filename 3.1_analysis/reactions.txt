# complex reactions with acid/base variations

cmplx_n: Complex Neutral
ethyl_benzoate      + water       -> benzoic_acid     + ethanol

cmplx_a: Complex Acidic
ethyl_benzoate(+)   + 2 water     -> benzoic_acid     + ethanol     + water(+)

cmplx_b: Complex Basic
ethyl_benzoate      + water(-)    -> benzoic_acid(-)  + ethanol

cmplx_a+b: Complex Acidic+Basic
ethyl_benzoate(+)   + water(-)    -> benzoic_acid     + ethanol

simp_b: Simple Basic
